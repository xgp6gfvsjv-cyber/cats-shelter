Adoption Journey

How Our Cat Adoption Works

Simple, friendly steps to help you find a safe, loving forever home for one of our cats.

  1. 1

    Browse & Shortlist

    Explore our featured cats and use the filters to find a kitten or adult cat that matches your lifestyle.

  2. 2

    Submit an Enquiry

    Tell us a little about your home, family and experience with pets so we can recommend a good match.

  3. 3

    Meet & Match

    Visit the shelter to spend time with your shortlisted cats, ask questions and check for a comfortable match.

  4. 4

    Prepare Your Home

    Set up a safe, quiet space with food, litter, scratching posts and cosy hiding spots before your cat comes home.

  5. 5

    Finalise Adoption

    Complete the paperwork, pay the adoption fee and take your new family member home with ongoing support from our team.

Adoption Requirements

  • 18 years or older with valid ID.
  • Stable housing with landlord or body corporate approval if renting.
  • Indoor-only or safely enclosed outdoor space for cats.
  • Ability to provide food, enrichment and regular vet care.
  • All household members agree to the adoption.
  • Willing to commit to a long-term, loving home.

Adoption Fees

Adoption fees help cover desexing, microchipping, vaccinations and health checks.

Kittens (under 6 months) $260
Adult cats (6 months+) $180
Bonded pair $320

Fees may vary for special needs or senior cats. Please contact us for current promotions and concessions.
