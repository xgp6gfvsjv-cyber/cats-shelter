Frequently Asked Questions

  • Absolutely, many of our cats are well suited to apartment living. We do require written approval from your landlord or body corporate, and we recommend keeping cats indoors or in a safely enclosed balcony or catio.

  • We offer a short trial period where you can bring the cat home and see how they settle in. If there are serious issues, please contact us as soon as possible and we will work with you to support the cat or arrange a return.
